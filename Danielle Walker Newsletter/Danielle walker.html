New Recipe: Instant Pot Spaghetti and Meatballs!
This one-pot Instant Pot Spaghetti and Meatballs has been a game-changer for me over the past few months. I used brown rice pasta, but I have included grain-free options for you as well!

With only 4 main ingredients, 6 if you count the salt and oil, this comes together in a matter of minutes, and only cooks for 6 minutes!

All in all, with the time it takes to come to pressure and gather your ingredients, you’re looking at about 20 minutes flat. Plus there’s only 1 pot to clean up at the end. As a full-time working mom of 3, this is like a unicorn meal.

Recipe Highlight: Vanilla Pineapple Margarita
This sweet and tropical Vanilla-Pineapple Margarita is an aromatic and fruity drink that’s ideal for these hot summer nights. I may have to add that they are deceptively strong, so be careful!

The pineapple mixed with the vanilla creates an incredibly smooth beverage. You can hardly taste the tequila in this margarita recipe, which can be a little dangerous!
